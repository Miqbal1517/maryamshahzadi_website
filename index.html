About Us

MIT Software Skills Institute was founded in 2023 by Mam Maryam and Muhammad Iqbal with a vision to empower students with practical IT skills. Since our beginning, we have been dedicated to delivering high-quality training in both online and offline modes.

We have successfully trained many students in various IT disciplines including Web Development (MERN Stack), Web Designing, Frontend Web Development, Backend Web Development, C/C++, Python, Computer Basics & MS Office (Word, PowerPoint, Excel), and Professional Computer Skills.

Mission Statement

"At MIT Software Skills Institute, our mission is to bridge the gap between education and technology, helping students build careers in the IT field. We believe in hands-on learning, mentorship, and creating opportunities for everyone to excel in the digital world."
